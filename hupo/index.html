Amber Weather

Providing precise weather forecast information, Amber Weather is serving millions of users all over the globe. Mass of cleverly-designed widgets are available from the in-app theme store to satisfy all the customized needs. By adding the widgets to desktop, various climate information including thermal temperature, wind speed, humidity, and air pressure are easily accessible by our users.

Amber Weather is built with multiple data sources in order to provide constant and precise weather information. Users are offered with real-time weather alert, air quality info, and historic weather data of the chosen area. Background picture of the app is changed automatically with the outside weather to give users a visualized view.
